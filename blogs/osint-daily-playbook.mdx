## Loop Template

1. Define question and boundary.
2. Collect from known trusted sources first.
3. Verify with at least one independent source.
4. Save evidence links and timestamps.
5. Write one paragraph summary.

> Keep every note evidence-first. Opinion comes after verification.

